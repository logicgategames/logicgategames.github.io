Privacy Policy

Folio - PDF Reader and Editor - LogicGateGames

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LogicGateGames collects, uses, and shares information when you use Folio. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information. Third-party services may collect data:

  • Google AdMob - Displays ads. May collect device identifiers and ad interaction data.
  • Google Play Billing - Handles in-app purchases. Payment data is managed by Google.
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Collects crash logs to help fix bugs. No personal data included.
  • ML Kit - On-device document scanning. No images sent to our servers.

2. PDF Files and Documents

All PDF files you use with Folio are processed entirely on your device. We do not upload or access your files.

3. Permissions

  • Storage - Required to open and save PDF files.
  • Camera - Used only for document scanning. Photos never uploaded.
  • Internet - Required for ads and in-app purchases.
  • Ad ID - Used by AdMob for relevant ads. Can be opted out in device settings.

4. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions and Remove Ads purchase are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive payment details.

5. Advertising

Free users see ads via Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalised ads in device Settings under Google - Ads. You can also purchase Remove Ads in the app.

6. Children

Folio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
