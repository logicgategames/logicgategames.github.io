3 On-Device AI Models

Scrivox uses several on-device AI models for speech recognition, summarization, speaker identification, and translation. Every one of them runs entirely on your device:

  • Base speech engine — OpenAI Whisper, an open-source model licensed under the MIT License. Downloaded automatically on first launch (~75 MB). Supports 71 languages including English, Spanish, French, and more.
  • Indian language packs — Fine-tuned Whisper models for Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, and Gujarati, developed by vasista22 (IIT Madras) and licensed under the MIT License. Optional and downloaded only when you choose to in Settings (~240–350 MB each).
  • AI summary model — Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct (Apache License 2.0), used to generate summaries and action items. Optional and downloaded only when you choose to (~1.7 GB).
  • Speaker identification — the pyannote.audio speaker-segmentation model (MIT License), used to label who spoke when. Optional and downloaded only when you choose to (~6 MB).
  • Translation & on-device generative AI — powered by Google ML Kit, including on-device translation models and, on supported devices, on-device summarization via Google's Gemini Nano through Android AICore. These process your text entirely on the device.

All model files are downloaded over HTTPS — the base engine from HuggingFace (huggingface.co), and the Indian language packs, AI summary model, and speaker model from GitHub Releases (github.com); ML Kit and Gemini Nano models are provided by Google Play services / Android AICore on your device. Once installed, all models work entirely offline. No audio, recordings, or transcript data is ever sent anywhere during transcription, summarization, speaker identification, or translation.

During a model download, the respective server (HuggingFace or GitHub) will receive your IP address as part of a standard HTTPS request. No audio, recordings, or personal data is transmitted. These downloads are governed by HuggingFace's Privacy Policy and GitHub's Privacy Policy. On-device processing by Google ML Kit is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.

4 Microphone Permission

Scrivox requests microphone access solely to record audio when you tap the record button. Audio is:

  • Captured only while you are actively recording
  • Processed on-device by the Whisper AI model
  • Stored as a local file on your device
  • Never transmitted over the internet

You can revoke permission anytime in Settings → Apps → Scrivox → Permissions.

5 Advertising (Free Tier)

The free version displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect data to serve personalised ads, including advertising ID, approximate location, and device information. See Google Privacy Policy. Upgrading to Premium removes all ads.

6 In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We receive only a transaction confirmation — we never see or store your payment details.

7 Data Retention & Deletion

All recordings and transcripts are stored on your device and fully under your control:

  • Delete individual recordings inside the app
  • Clear all data via Settings → Clear All Recordings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data

8 Children's Privacy

Scrivox is intended for adults (18 and over) and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
