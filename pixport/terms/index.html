PixPort is an on-device image utility that crops, resizes, and compresses photos and signatures to match publicly published file specifications (such as 630 × 810 px / under 250 KB for photos, and under 100 KB for signatures) used by the Indian passport application portal. It also runs advisory quality checks (face position, background, lighting, etc.).
