Privacy Policy — PixPort

Last updated: July 2026 · LogicGateGames LLC

Important: PixPort is an independent utility app. It is NOT a government application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or approved by Passport Seva, the Ministry of External Affairs, or the Government of India. PixPort only helps you prepare image files to published size specifications; acceptance of any file is determined solely by the official portal.

Summary

PixPort processes all photos and signatures entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your photos, signatures, or any personal data. There are no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Photos and signature images — processed locally; never uploaded to any server
  • Face detection data — Google ML Kit runs on-device only; no biometric data leaves your phone
  • Personal information — no accounts, no names, no email addresses
  • Usage analytics — no analytics SDKs are included
  • Advertising identifiers — the app contains no advertising SDKs

2. Data Stored On Your Device Only

  • Processed images — saved to your device gallery (Pictures/PassportPhoto) only when you tap Save
  • Upload counter — a simple number stored in the app's local preferences to help you track the portal's 12-upload limit
  • Temporary files — processed images are cached in the app's private cache directory, cleared by the system automatically

3. Permissions

  • Photos/Media access — to let you pick a photo from your gallery and save the result
  • Internet — used only by the operating system to install Google ML Kit's on-device face model at app install time, and to open this policy in your browser. The app itself sends no data.

4. Third-Party Services

PixPort uses Google ML Kit Face Detection (on-device). Face detection is performed entirely on your phone; images are not sent to Google. See Google ML Kit terms.

5. Children

PixPort does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data at all.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
