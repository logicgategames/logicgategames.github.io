Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026 · Applies to the WAKEM Android app (package: com.logicgategames.wakem), published by LogicGateGames LLC.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information WAKEM collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. WAKEM is a mission-based alarm clock and sleep tracking app. We built it to keep as much as possible on your device rather than on our servers, and this policy tells you exactly where that's true and where it isn't.

Information We Collect

Data stored only on your device. The following is stored locally in WAKEM's own database and is never transmitted to us or any server we control:

  • Alarms you create — time, label, repeat days, sound choice, snooze settings, and which wake-up missions you've selected
  • Sleep log entries — when you started and ended sleep, and the quality rating you give each night
  • Morning routine checklist items and completion status
  • Wake-up mission statistics — which missions you've completed and how long they took
  • App settings — dark mode, bedtime reminder time, premium status

If you uninstall WAKEM, this data is deleted along with the app, unless your device's own backup system (e.g. Android's automatic backup) restores it on reinstall.

Camera. Two optional wake-up mission types — Photo and Scan Barcode — use your device's camera. See the dedicated section below.

Advertising identifier. WAKEM shows ads through Google AdMob (unless you're a Premium subscriber or have purchased the one-time ad removal). AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and other technical identifiers to serve and measure ads. See the Advertising section below.

Purchase information. Subscriptions and one-time purchases are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation that a purchase was made and its product ID, but we never see your payment card details — those are handled directly by Google.

Camera & Wake-Up Missions

If you choose the Photo mission, WAKEM asks you to register a photo of a spot in your home (like a bathroom sink) and, when the alarm rings, take a new photo of that same spot to dismiss it. If you choose the Scan Barcode mission, you register a barcode (like a product in your kitchen) and scan it to dismiss the alarm.

Both missions process images entirely on your device using on-device image matching / barcode scanning. Photos and scanned images are never uploaded, stored on a server, or seen by us. The camera permission is used only while a mission is actively in progress.

Advertising

WAKEM uses Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads to free users. AdMob may use your advertising identifier and other data to serve ads and measure their performance. You can review how Google handles this data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Where required (for example under GDPR in the EU/UK), WAKEM presents a consent form before showing personalized ads, using Google's User Messaging Platform. You can reset or change your ad preferences at any time from your device's system settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on most Android versions), or by resetting your advertising ID.

Removing ads — either through the one-time purchase or a Premium subscription — stops AdMob requests entirely for your account going forward.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

WAKEM offers a monthly Premium subscription and a one-time ad-removal purchase, both processed by Google Play Billing under your Google Play account. We don't collect or store your payment method, billing address, or card details. Purchase management, cancellation, and refunds are handled through the Google Play Store app, not within WAKEM itself.

Data Storage & Security

WAKEM does not currently require an account or sign-in, and does not sync your data to a cloud server. Everything listed under "Information We Collect" above lives in a local database on your device, protected by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your information. The only outside parties WAKEM shares data with are the service providers necessary to run the app:

  • Google AdMob — to serve advertising, as described above
  • Google Play Billing — to process purchases and subscriptions

Each operates under its own privacy policy, and neither receives your locally-stored alarm, sleep, or routine data — that never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

WAKEM is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can revoke the camera or notification permission at any time in your device's app settings; this will disable the Photo/Barcode missions or alarm notifications respectively.
  • You can delete any alarm, sleep log entry, or routine item directly within the app.
  • Uninstalling WAKEM removes all locally-stored data described above.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, or deletion requests) under laws like the GDPR or CCPA. Contact us using the details below to exercise them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
