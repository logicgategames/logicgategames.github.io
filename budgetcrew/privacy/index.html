Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BudgetCrew ("the app," "we," "us") handles information. BudgetCrew is developed and published by LogicGateGames LLC ("LogicGateGames," "we," "us"), a Michigan, USA limited liability company.

The short version: BudgetCrew has no user accounts and no server that stores your financial data. Everything you enter — transactions, budgets, goals, receipts — stays on your device unless you deliberately choose to sync it directly with another device you control, or export it yourself.

What stays on your device

BudgetCrew is built to work entirely offline for your financial data. The following is stored only in the app's private storage on your device, and is never sent to us or to any server we operate:

  • Transactions, categories, budgets, savings goals, debts, recurring payments, shopping lists, and warranty records you create
  • Photos of receipts you choose to attach to a transaction
  • App preferences and settings, such as your chosen theme, currency, and reminder times

We do not operate a cloud database of user financial data. There is nothing on a LogicGateGames server for us to access, because nothing is sent there in the first place.

Device-to-device sync

BudgetCrew includes an optional feature that lets you share a budget space directly between your own devices, or with another person's device, over Bluetooth or a local Wi-Fi connection. This feature:

  • Is off unless you turn it on and actively pair with another device
  • Transfers data directly between the paired devices — it is not uploaded to or relayed through a LogicGateGames server
  • Lets you choose which budget spaces, if any, a given paired device can see
  • Uses Google Play Services' Nearby Connections capability, which runs on your device, to help two nearby devices discover and connect to each other

Backups and exports

You can create a backup file or export your transactions (as CSV, Excel, or PDF) at any time. These files are saved to your device or shared through your device's normal share options — for example, to your own cloud storage, email, or messaging apps of your choosing. What happens to a file after you export and share it is governed by wherever you choose to send it, not by this app.

Third-party services

BudgetCrew uses a small number of well-known third-party services to operate. Each is provided by Google and governed by Google's own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — displays ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect an advertising identifier and interaction data to select and measure ads, as described in Google's advertising privacy policy. Ads are not shown to users with an active premium subscription.
  • Google Play Billing — processes optional in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment details are handled by Google Play; BudgetCrew never sees or stores your payment information.
  • Google ML Kit — powers the optional receipt scanner. Text recognition runs on your device; a scanned receipt image is not uploaded to Google or to us for this purpose.
  • Google Play Services (Nearby Connections) — the on-device component that enables the device-to-device sync feature described above.

We do not use any analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising service beyond AdMob, and we do not sell your information to anyone.

Permissions the app requests

Android requires apps to declare why they need certain capabilities. Here's what each permission BudgetCrew may request is actually for:

  • Internet / network state — needed to show ads and to download the on-device text-recognition model the first time it's used.
  • Notifications — needed to show reminders and budget alerts you've enabled.
  • Nearby devices / Bluetooth / Wi-Fi state — needed only for the optional device-to-device sync feature described above, to find and connect to another device. These permissions are explicitly flagged to Android as not used to determine your location, and BudgetCrew does not use them for that purpose.
  • Location (older Android versions only) — on Android 12 and earlier, the operating system requires this permission to be present for Bluetooth device discovery to function at all. BudgetCrew does not read or store your location; this permission exists solely because of that OS-level requirement, and is not requested at all on newer Android versions.

Children's privacy

BudgetCrew is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since BudgetCrew does not have accounts or a server-side data store, there is no cloud repository of user data for a child's information to end up in.

Your choices and control

  • You can delete any transaction, category, or other record at any time within the app.
  • You can turn device-to-device sync off, or revoke a paired device's access to a specific budget space, at any time.
  • Uninstalling the app removes its locally stored data from your device, subject to your device's own backup settings (for example, Android's system-level app backup, which is separate from anything BudgetCrew controls).
  • You can request removal of your Google Play purchase history through Google's own account tools, since that data is held by Google, not by us.

International users

Because your financial data is stored on your own device rather than on a central server, it does not routinely cross borders as part of using the app. Data you deliberately export and share yourself is subject to the destination you choose.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time, for example as we add new features. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
